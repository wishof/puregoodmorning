"WHAT IS THE HISTORY OF GOOD MORNING?"

The phrase "good morning" has been used as a monial for centuries, but its exact origin is unclear. It is likely that people have been using similar monials since ancient times, as a way to acknowledge and express goodwill towards one another at the start of a new day!
